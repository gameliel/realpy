What do we do?
fibiachomes on-boards owners and their houses and rents them out to tenants. fibiachomes provides convenience to the owner in terms of showing the house to potential tenants, rent collection and service and maintenance of the property.

List My Property

How do I reach out to fibiachomes to list my property?

Visit www.fibiachomes.com and click list your property on the “Homepage” or call us on 070 8778 9815 to give us your details. Once you share your details, one of our representatives will get in touch with you and take you forward on the Listing.

How much does it cost to list my property?

Nothing. It’s free to list with fibiachomes.
What’s more? We look for tenants for you.

Steps to list with fibiachomes

Step 1. You share your details with fibiachomes
Step 2. fibiachomes performs a Property inspection
Step 3. We sign the Agreement and begin on-boarding your house on fibiachomes
Step 4. We take professional photographs of your house and publish the house on our website
Step 5. Tenants express interest and schedule a visit to your house
Step 6. Once your house matches a tenant’s expectation, the tenant completes our verification form and submits the required documents. We carry out due diligence on tenant to determine tenant’s eligibility. We submit the eligibility result to you for your final approval if tenant is eligible
Step 7: We draft the tenancy agreement to be signed by tenant and you
Step 8: We transfer rent to you
